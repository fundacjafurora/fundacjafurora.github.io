Organize an Erasmus Plus + project with us
We are a trusted partner with whom you will implement your Erasmus + mobility project from start to finish


WHY IS IT WORTH WORKING WITH US?

Coordination of European programs

Our team will propose solutions and project organization tailored to the partner's needs. After determining the number of participants, their needs and preferences for a specific place, we help in writing the project. At a later stage, we are responsible for organizing a stay abroad, finding the right stakeholders, accommodation, meals and organizing the entire logistics of the project for the group. All this is in accordance with the requirements of the project and the preferences of the shipping unit.

Organization of documents

European mobility programs require the completion of many documents before, after and during the organization of a project. Our coordinators will make sure that all formalities are fulfilled. Contracts, insurance and certificates are our everyday life.

Travel and transportation

Travel is an inseparable element of Erasmus + programs, therefore our team makes sure that all participants and carers can reach their place of stay and internship without any problems. For each project, we will propose convenient solutions for public transport and excursions.

Rich cultural program 

Travel, discover and learn. During your stay abroad, you cannot miss learning about the culture and sightseeing tours. Therefore, we guarantee many different options tailored to the group
