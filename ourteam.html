OUR TEAM

MARCIN TEMPCZYK
President

Co-founder and president of the Furora Foundation. He has several years of experience in career counseling, which he uses to work with young people in non-governmental organizations. He is a graduate of industrial engineering and management, in his career he was responsible for managing, organizing and controlling project activities, teaching management techniques and making strategic decisions. He is also a cybersecurity specialist and currently works as an analyst at one of the largest professional services networks in the world - EY.

NATALIA BRODZIK
Vice President

Co-founder and vice-president of the Furora Foundation. For several years, she has been active in the NGO sector, participated in about 20 workshops and trainings related to entrepreneurship, personal development, leadership and activation of young people. She is a student of management and new technologies, and currently works as an HR specialist at Morgan Advertising, thanks to which she is very familiar with the needs of the labor market and the requirements of employers.
