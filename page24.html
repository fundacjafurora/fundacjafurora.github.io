WHAT ARE WE DOING?

ABOUT US

_____

The Furora Foundation was born out of motivation, hard work and a sense of purpose, and the inspiration for its establishment was the experience gained during many trainings and events. It was created by participants in international projects, social activists and initiators of local activities. We all have interesting and valuable experience in action - we were involved in volunteering, the work of non-governmental organizations and public institutions. Together, we decided to shape the local reality in which we live and live and to enable other young people to travel abroad to exchange experiences and inspire each other with our peers from different countries.

We create initiatives under Erasmus +, European Solidarity Corps and other local, regional and national grants. We carry out, among others international exchanges, social campaigns, trainings, workshops, debates and conferences. We focus on the topics of personal development, building leadership attitudes, intercultural integration and entrepreneurship.
We are happy to share our experience by conducting workshops and training others. At the same time, we constantly learn new things and set new challenges for ourselves.
The main method we use to achieve our goals is non-formal education, i.e. learning through practice and experience. Our initiatives are aimed at young people, people with fewer opportunities and all those who feel the need to act for the benefit of others, influence the environment and develop their own skills.
We work with other non-governmental organizations from around the world, media, public authorities, politicians, journalists, volunteers, experts, trainers, facilitators and representatives of various professions and areas of life, depending on the initiative.
