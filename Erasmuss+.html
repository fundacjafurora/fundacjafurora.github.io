Why is it worth to go?


New friendships
A great opportunity to get to know other cultures
The best way to learn languages
Unforgettable memories

Our goal is to create unique projects for you in the form of international exchanges, training courses, workshops, debates, trainings and conferences. Through their implementation, we promote the idea of ​​non-formal education and lifelong learning. Thanks to active participation, you can deepen your knowledge in many areas, including personal development, leadership, intercultural dialogue, critical thinking, entrepreneurship and the fight against discrimination. Participation in the project is an excellent investment in yourself and your future. It allows you to get to know your strengths and weaknesses, teaches humility, tolerance, is a test of our character or an opportunity to discover new skills and passions. It is a real chance to realize what really interests you and what you would like to do in the future. Participation in projects may also pay off in your professional career - if included in your CV, the employer will perceive it well. It means that you are not afraid of challenges and that you can cope quickly in the new environment. You will also have a unique chance to get to know many innovative methods and tools of non-formal education, e.g. design thinking, storytelling, roleplay, case study, etc. Thanks to our initiatives you will also meet many interesting people and representatives of different cultures, which is a great, unusual and unique experience. Nothing enriches and broadens horizons as real contact with other customs. The ability to act in an international team and learn to constructively solve potential conflicts will surely impress the future employer!

5 steps to departure

1

1

Choose the project that interests you

Information about current projects can be found on our home page and on our social networks. Detailed information on each project can be found in the attached info pack, which you should read carefully. Make the decision to leave, guided primarily by your interests.
2

Check out the requirements

All participants should know and accept the requirements for their participation in the project, involvement and post-project activities.
3

Complete the application form

You will find a link to the application form under the description of each project. Please make sure that all required fields on the form have been correctly completed.
4

Buy tickets

After you qualify for the project, you have 3 days to purchase tickets to travel or fly to the project site. Choose the most ecological and economical mode of transport and send your chosen travel option for approval.
5

Set off on an adventure!

Integration evenings, where traditional dishes and customs are presented, are an important part of each exchange, so remember to bring refreshments from your home country for international participants.
